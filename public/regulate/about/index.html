About.

This is an essential project.

The number of adults whose nervous systems are dysregulated is no longer a fringe statistic. It is the baseline state of working life in most developed economies. Cognitive load is at historic highs. Sleep is deteriorating. Anxiety and depression is rising across every age cohort. The structures that used to provide coherence — stable careers, predictable institutions, intelligible economies, durable identities — are dissolving faster than most people can adapt to.

The work here is the response to that. To put one practical instrument — the breath — into the hands of people who need it, without any of the things that would normally come with a wellness brand: no streaks, no gamification, no infinite optimization.

A book. A weekly letter. The Return app. The intent is that more people can live more coherently in an accelerating world. That is the whole purpose.

Christoph Kneip
