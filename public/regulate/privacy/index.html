Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Your privacy is paramount. Return ("the App") is designed around the principle of Privacy by Design. This Privacy Policy explains how we interact with data when you use the App and our website hosted via GitHub Pages (the "Service").

We comply with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP/DSG) and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

Since Return is operated by a private individual based in Zürich, Switzerland, you can contact the data controller directly regarding any privacy inquiries at: info@returnapp.org

2. Core App Data: Local-Only Storage

When you use Return to log breathing sessions or track your daily mood, this data is saved exclusively on your device's local storage.

  • No Server Uploads: We do not collect, view, store, or transmit your breathing logs or mood data to any external servers.
  • Your Control: Within the App, you have the built-in capability to instantly delete all your data or download your complete history in a standard .csv file.

3. Data Collected by Third Parties

While we do not personally collect your data, the App utilizes standard third-party developer tools and ecosystems (Apple and Google) to function, process payments, and improve performance. These third parties collect data according to their own privacy policies:

Provider Purpose Data Type Collected
Apple App Store In-App Purchases & Distribution Transaction data, device identifiers, basic account metrics.
Google Play Store / Google Analytics In-App Purchases & Telemetry Transaction data, crash logs, device model, and aggregate usage statistics (e.g., session duration).
GitHub Pages Website Hosting Standard server logs (including visitor IP addresses) collected automatically for security and network performance.

4. Website Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics in the future to optimize performance and analyze traffic. If implemented, these tools will respect your browser's "Do Not Track" signals, and you will be prompted with a cookie consent banner where required by law.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is restricted to individuals aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information (via support emails, for instance), we will delete it immediately.

6. Your Legal Rights (FADP & GDPR)

Depending on your residency (such as Switzerland or the European Economic Area), you possess the following rights regarding data processing:

  • Right to Access: You can export all your App data anytime via the local .csv download feature.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all your App data instantly via the settings inside the App.
  • Right to Information: You can contact us at info@returnapp.org to ask what data (if any, such as support emails) we have concerning you.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this page periodically for any modifications.

8. Contact Information

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, your rights, or our data handling practices, please reach out to us at: info@returnapp.org
