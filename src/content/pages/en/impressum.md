## Health Disclaimer

The information on this website is offered for educational and informational purposes only. It is not intended as, and must not be treated as, medical or psychological advice. The author is not a licensed medical practitioner.

Conscious breathing practices are generally safe for healthy adults but may be contraindicated for certain conditions, including but not limited to: pregnancy, cardiovascular disease, uncontrolled hypertension, severe respiratory conditions, recent surgery, panic disorder under active treatment, and certain neurological conditions.

If you have a medical or mental health condition, are pregnant, or are unsure whether a breath practice is appropriate for you, please consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning.

In any emergency or acute crisis, contact your local emergency services.
